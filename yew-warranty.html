What's Not Covered

Our warranty is comprehensive, but it does not cover issues arising from external factors or misuse.

  • Improper installation or failure to follow setup instructions.
  • Damage caused by battery leakage, misuse, or accidents.
  • Unauthorized repairs, modifications, or disassembly.
  • Normal wear and tear, including cosmetic scratches or fading.
  • Damage from fire, flood, or other acts of God.

A Note on Batteries

Please use high-quality, new alkaline batteries. Damage resulting from battery leakage (corrosion) is the most common issue not covered by warranty. Replace all batteries at the same time.

Frequently Asked Questions

How do I find my serial number?

Your Yew lock's serial number is located on a sticker inside the battery compartment cover. It is typically a 12-digit alphanumeric code.

Is the warranty transferable?

No, the warranty is non-transferable and applies only to the original purchaser of the product from an authorized Yew retailer.

Do I need to keep my receipt?

Yes, a valid proof of purchase (such as a sales receipt or invoice) is required to validate all warranty claims.
